My name is Alba Bernal.
I'm from Malaga but currently I'm living in Barcelona.
I graduated in Fine Arts at the University of Barcelona in 2009 and have attended several Design and Illustration Workshops and a postgraduate degree in Design. Since then I've worked as an illustrator and graphic designer. I've also participated in exhibitions in France, Barcelona and Malaga.
As a designer I've worked from traditional graphic design to specialize in UX/UI in the area of digital product. I love research, doing interviews and surveys, and making high and low fidelity prototypes. I believe that usability is a philosophy of life that allows you to know and make your environment easier. It's the warmest part of my work and that's why I adore it.
About Illustration, I use traditional techniques and digital techniques, although what I like most is to combine both.
I like living near the sea, French food, the Surrealist movement and of course cats! Which are things that inspire my creative work. Other things that I like and inspire me are children's books, science, psychology and human behaviour.
All my works are done with love I hope you enjoy it!
Thank you for visiting!:)
